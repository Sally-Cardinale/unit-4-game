Instructions:

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to a random result selected by the computer. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random result.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the values of the random result and each crystal will change.

Once you win (or lose) your wins and losses will show in the Wins/Losses box.
